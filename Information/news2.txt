Title:How ChatGPT and AI are transforming education

Subtitle:The transformation of education by ChatGPT and AI can be seen in various ways. These technologies are bringing in innovative changes to the traditional education system, making it more engaging, personalised, and accessible to students all over the world.

News data:

Artificial Intelligence has been around for decades, but its impact on education has been limited due to the lack of accessibility and affordability. However, in the last 4 months, access to AI and ChatGPT has become more widespread and affordable, creating a huge wave in education.

The pandemic forced educators to adopt new and innovative teaching methods to engage with their students during remote learning. However, without intuitive tools, both teachers and students struggle.
